How to Draw a Chicken

STEP ONE:


Start by drawing the outline of the chicken. You can draw it all together, or break it down by combining each of these shapes:


  1. Body: a large semi-circle
  2. Neck: a long rectangle extending from the body
  3. Head an oval that is attached to the neck
  4. Beak: a triangle coming from the head
  5. Wattle: a triangle with rounded corners placed below the beak
  6. Head feathers:Head feathers: three smaller rectangles sticking out from the top of the head
  7. Tail feathers: three rectangles sticking out from the end of the body
  8. Feet: a line with three smaller lines extending out from it
