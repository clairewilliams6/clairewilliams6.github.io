How to Draw a Chicken

STEP THREE:


Add a funky pattern to the chicken body, neck, and head.


I opted to create a grid-like pattern for the body, and drew varying-sized semi-circles to imitate feathers on the neck and head. You can draw any fun pattern or shape you like, such as lines, polka dots, circles, etc.
